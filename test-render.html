  • Votes and Hangs
    This tab covers voting, unvoting, No Hangs, and hangs.
    \n
    \n \n
      \n
    • Votes must be in this format to be counted: Vote: [player username].
      • \n
    • By default, votes should be made in [b] (bold) tags. However, votes made in [code] and [c] tags will also be accepted.
      • \n
    • Usernames in votes must be spelled, but not necessarily capitalized, correctly. Do not use nicknames.
      • \n
    • For the purposes of voting, I (capital i) and l (lowercase L) are considered identical.
      • \n
    • For example, both Vote: mathjay and Vote: mAtHjAy are valid votes, but vote: mathjay, Vote: jay, Vote: mathjay., Vote:mathjay, Vote: mathjay, and Vote: mathjay are invalid.
      • \n
    • Votes followed by extraneous punctuation that is similarly formatted, even with separate tags, are invalid.
      • \n
    • Votes in hide, tip, or quote tags do not count.
      • \n
    • Vote to hang no one by posting Vote: No Hang.
      • \n
    • Unvote by posting Unvote. You do not have to unvote before voting again.
      • \n
    • Votecounts will be posted periodically. Players may request a new VC in bold or in their role PM.
      • \n
    • Invalid votes will not count under any circumstances. Consider notifying the mods in your role PM if you want to purposely fakevote.
      • \n
    • Valid votes will always be counted unless they are clearly unintentional. There is no guarantee that an accidental vote will not be counted.
      • \n
    • Votes will be carried over if a player is replaced.
      • \n
    • The Not Voting section should be ignored if it contradicts another part of the votecount (VC).
      • \n
    • If an error in the votecount is not pointed out within 2 days or by the next EOD, whichever is sooner, the incorrect VC will become official.
      • \n
    • This game uses a hybrid hang system. If a player is voted by the majority of living players, then that player will be hanged immediately; and if No Hang is voted by half of living players, a No Hang will occur.
      • \n
    • If the deadline is met and no player has been hanged, the amount of votes required for a Hang or a No Hang will be reduced by 1. The vote threshold will reduce by 1 vote every 24 hours after this. For example, if there are 8 players alive in the game, 5 votes will be required to hang someone and 4 votes are required for a no hang. Once the deadline passes, 4 votes will be sufficient to hang someone and 3 votes are sufficient for a no hang. 24 hours after the deadline, 3 votes will be sufficient to hang someone and 2 votes are sufficient for a no hang.
      • \n
    • If the vote threshold is decreased and two or more options have both reached the threshold required, then the game will enter tiebreak. During tiebreak, votes can continue to be changed. If at any point in time, only one option has enough votes to meet the threshold required for the option to be enacted, day will immediately end. Or, if any option gains votes and now exceeds the vote threshold by an amount of votes strictly greater than the amount of votes any other option exceeds their vote threshold by, that option will win and day will immediately end. If 24 hours have passed since tiebreak has begun, and the votes are still deadlocked, there will be no hang.
      • \n
    • Upon reaching LYLO (Hang-or-Lose), votes will be locked, meaning that they cannot be changed.
      • \n
    • Deadline hang is off at LYLO. If a majority hang is not reached by the deadline, the day will end without a hang, resulting in an automatic win for the mafia team.
      • \n
    • If a hammer has occurred and the EOD has not been declared, the game will proceed into twilight. Players may still post, but no more votes or unvotes will be counted.
      • \n
    • Once the moderators declare the EOD, the person hanged will be officially eliminated and their role revealed. Night will then immediately begin.

  • Activity
    This tab covers deadlines, activity rules, V/LAs, and replacements.
    \n
    \n \n
      \n
    • All times will be in Pacific Standard Time (PST, UTC-8) or Pacific Daylight Time (PDT, UTC-7) as applicable, unless otherwise noted.
      • \n
    • Each day phase will be 192 hours (8 days), and each night phase will be 48 hours (2 days).
      • \n
    • The moderators may extend deadlines at their discretion.
      • \n
    • The current deadline can be found in the bottom of the OP (Original Post, or post #1).
      • \n
    • If the mafia anticipates being unavailable during the night phase, they may submit any night actions to be performed in advance.
      • \n
    • If the mafia has not submitted their action by 24 hours before the night deadline, a reminder will be sent.
      • \n
    • If the moderators do not receive an NA submission from any mafia by the night deadline, the mafia will not perform an action that night.
      • \n
    • Night will end when the moderators make a post declaring the end of night (EON). You may not post in the thread until then, even if the night deadline has passed. Instead, PM the mods for updates if they miss a deadline.
      • \n
    • Players who are temporarily unable to fulfill activity requirements can declare a V/LA (Vacation/Long Absence) by posting the V/LA dates in bold in-thread or in their role PM. Players on a V/LA will not be prodded.
      • \n
    • V/LAs should be shorter than five days. For longer absences, contact the moderators for options.
      • \n
    • Players who no longer wish to or are unable to play in this game can request replacement by posting Request Replacement in-thread or in their role PM.
      • \n
    • Players who have replaced out generally will not be allowed to replace back in.
      • \n
    • Players will receive their first PM prod if they have not made a game-advancing post for 48 hours and are not on V/LA. Details on "game-advancing post" are in the Moderation Policies tab.
      • \n
    • Players will receive their second PM prod if they have not made a game-advancing post for 72 hours and are not on V/LA.
      • \n
    • Players will be replaced if they have not made a game-advancing post for 96 hours and are not on V/LA, unless they post before the moderators find a replacement.
      • \n
    • If a player receives 4 PM Prods, they will be replaced.
      • \n
    • If a player reaches replacement status more than once, they will be replaced.
      • \n
    • Activity Checks (ACs) will be posted periodically. Players may request a new AC in bold or in their role PM.
      • \n
    • Activity will reset overnight. Players will not receive prods between twilight and daystart.
      • \n
    • If you have access to a private topic, you may request that a partner be poked. The moderators will send a notification via PM to the specified player, but this will not count as an official prod.
      • \n
    • If a player is postbanned by the AoPS Sheriff, they will be replaced. Details are in the Moderation Policies tab.\n
